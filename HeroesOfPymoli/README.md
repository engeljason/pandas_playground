
# HeroesOfPymoli

## Overview

Analyze transaction data from a game and display various result tables

## Observations

* The primary demographics are men (84% overall) and the 20-24 year old age group (45% overall)
* Most buyers were not repeat buyers
  * Average purchases per buyer was 1.3
* Repeat buyers were more willing to pay higher prices
  * Average purchase price of top 5 repeat customers was $4.01 vs $3.05 overall
* Most items had multiple purchases
  * 94% of items had a sales count greater than 1
  * The mean and median sales counts were both ~4.0
